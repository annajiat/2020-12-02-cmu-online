Who: Learners are 32 graduate students, postdocs, and staff at CMU from a variety of disciplines. The main disciplines will be Social Sciences and Biomedical Engineering. They want to learn Python and have been on a waiting list to take this class. They will have a variety of experience programming. For their graduate work, their data will typically be in CSV or Excel spreadsheets (making JSON and SQL less applicable.) You don't need to have any previous knowledge of the tools that will be presented at the workshop.
